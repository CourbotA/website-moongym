Kru Tik Palmares

Legend Lumpini champion and world champion with decades of top-level Muay Thai experience.

Disciplines: Muay Thai and Muay Boran.

  • 1985: Lumpini Stadium Bantamweight Champion, Thailand
  • 1986: Lumpini Stadium Bantamweight Champion, Thailand
  • 1987: Lumpini Featherweight Champion, Thailand
  • 1989: World Champion Junior Lightweight, France
  • 1995: TV Channel 7 Lightweight Champion, Thailand
  • 2016: Muay Thai Ambassador of the Year, Sport Authority of Thailand
  • 2022: Award for Conserving and Disseminating the Art of Muay Thai

Kru Noom Resume

A highly experienced Muay Thai trainer who has competed in Thailand’s top stadiums and brings real fight experience into every session.

  • Stadium Experience: Rajadamnern Stadium, Lumpinee Stadium, Omnoi Stadium
  • Achievements: Runner-up at Lumpinee Stadium
  • Placement: 3rd place at Lumpinee Stadium (105-118 lbs division)
  • Fight Record: Over 200 professional fights
  • Notable Wins: NongNarong Luksamrong, Rattanachai Wor.Warapol, Lamnammoon S.Sumalee

Kru Artnarong B.K.S. (Kru Art) Resume

An experienced Muay Thai trainer with a strong background in Thailand’s top professional stadiums, bringing real fight knowledge into every training session.

  • Stadium Experience: Rajadamnern Stadium, Lumpinee Stadium, Omnoi Stadium

FAQ

Questions Before Booking

Quick answers for first-time visitors, long-stay students, and returning fighters.

Can beginners train at Moon Fight?

Yes. Sessions are beginner-friendly and coaches scale intensity based on your level and fitness.

How do I book a class or private session?

The fastest way is WhatsApp. Send your available day, your training level, and preferred session type.

What should I bring for training?

Bring water, towel, and training clothes. Ask on WhatsApp if you need help with equipment planning.

Do you offer private Muay Thai training?

Yes. Private coaching is available Monday to Saturday between 11:00 and 15:00.

Where is the gym located in Koh Phangan?

The Moon Fight Muay Thai Gym is in Koh Phangan, Surat Thani, Thailand. Full map link is in the contact section.
